Nani’s Journey and “Hit 3” Success – A Quick Glance


Nani, also known as the “Natural Star” of Telugu cinema, began his career as a radio jockey before stepping into films. He made his acting debut with Ashta Chamma (2008) and quickly gained recognition for his natural acting style and relatable roles. Over the years, Nani built a strong fan base with hits like Eega, Bhale Bhale Magadivoy, Jersey, and Shyam Singha Roy.

In 2024, Nani delivered a major blockbuster with "Hit: The Third Case (Hit 3)", a gripping crime thriller that won both critical acclaim and audience love. His intense performance and the movie’s engaging narrative contributed to its massive box office success, solidifying Nani’s position as a versatile actor and bankable star.
